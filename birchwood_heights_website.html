Meet the Women of Birchwood Heights

🎃 Lyndi Bailey

The sunshine teacher who makes chaos feel like home

Art teacher and foster mom to nephew Micah (13). Holiday decorator, sings off-key, hot-glues everything.

"October isn't complete without glitter."

🔧 Oakley Meadows

The grump in work boots who secretly loves midnight pies

Mechanic and single mom to Claudia. Owns too many cardigans, horror novel addict, allergic to gossip.

"I'm not carving a pumpkin. Don't ask."

🍂 Lindsay Hunter

The runaway perfectionist learning to rebuild

Shelter mom of twins Emma & Ethan. Fierce protector, stress-cleans, turns canned goods into feasts.

"Fake dating seemed easier than explaining my life."

🦋 Devyn Prescott

The prodigal daughter who never thought she'd find love again

Volunteer and widowed "black sheep" returnee. Wry humor, writes unsent letters, takes every thankless job.

"I came back for the kids. I stayed because of you."

🎄 Annie Ames

The perfectionist who has to learn love can't be scheduled

PTA queen and recently divorced. Color-coded binders, hums carols when stressed, pageant-obsessed.

"If it's not perfect, it's not happening."

✨ Emberlynn Walters

The whirlwind who turns pageants — and Annie's world — upside down

Chaotic kindergarten teacher. Loves glitter bombs, burns cookies, adopts stray cats.

"You don't need perfect. You need real. And real is messy."

About Claerie Kavanaugh

Claerie writes cozy sapphic romances full of found family, small-town charm, and happily ever afters. When not crafting love stories, she can be found collecting vintage teacups and planning elaborate holiday decorations.
